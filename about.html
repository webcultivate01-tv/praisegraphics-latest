About PraiseGraphics

PraiseGraphics is a creative design brand focused on building powerful and visually compelling brand identities for individuals, businesses, and organizations. The brand is dedicated to transforming ideas into professional and engaging visuals that communicate purpose, attract attention, and elevate how brands are perceived.

PraiseGraphics doesn't just create designs — it builds visual experiences that help brands stand out, connect with their audience, and grow with confidence.

The Journey

Our Story

What started as a one-person freelance hustle in 2019 has grown into one of Nigeria's most trusted creative studios — and this is just the beginning.

Praise Graphics was born out of frustration. Too many Nigerian businesses were being sold generic, low-quality designs that failed to represent their true potential. Praise Okonkwo decided to change that.

Starting with just a laptop, Adobe Creative Suite, and an unwavering belief in the power of great design, the studio began taking on clients through referrals. Word spread fast — not because of marketing, but because the work genuinely transformed businesses.

Today, with 150+ completed projects across Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, and beyond, Praise Graphics continues to be the studio that Nigerian entrepreneurs trust when they're ready to get serious about their brand.
